# Senior Checkpoint Test

Take this test around the middle of the level or after a major architecture block.

## Test Goal

Check whether you are thinking like a platform owner instead of only an implementer.

## Tasks

1. Write a design proposal for one data platform subsystem.
2. Compare at least 2 architecture options and justify the final choice.
3. Define reliability, observability, and cost considerations.
4. Describe how access control or governance is handled.
5. Propose standards another engineer could follow.
6. Review one existing design and identify 3 likely failure points.

## Pass Standard

You pass if you can:

- defend tradeoffs clearly
- connect technical decisions to business impact
- reason about operations, not just implementation
- improve the clarity of work for other engineers

## If You Fail

Spend 2 to 4 more weeks on:

- architecture writing
- observability and incident review
- cost and scale tradeoffs
- technical mentoring and design review
