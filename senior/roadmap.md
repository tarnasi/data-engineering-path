# Senior Mastery Roadmap

## Purpose

This level is about architecture, standards, tradeoffs, and leadership.
You are not just shipping pipelines.
You are improving the whole system and the way the team works.

## Time

- Target: 32 to 52 weeks
- This level is ongoing
- Daily work: 2.5 to 4 hours of deep work, review, or design

## Scope

Focus on:

- platform architecture
- cost and reliability tradeoffs
- observability and incident response
- governance and access patterns
- streaming and CDC foundations where relevant
- migration planning
- mentoring and review standards

## Task Mapping In This Repository

Use the Senior task files only after Mid mastery is stable.
These tasks are not tutorials.
They should be treated as design and ownership work.

## Weekly Structure

- Day 1: architecture and long-term design
- Day 2: platform quality and observability
- Day 3: performance, scale, or cost work
- Day 4: review, standards, and mentoring
- Day 5: cross-system reliability or migration planning
- Day 6: write design docs and postmortem-quality reviews
- Day 7: rest or light review

## Mastery Targets

You should be able to:

- design systems with clear tradeoffs
- raise platform reliability and maintainability
- define standards others can follow
- lead technical decisions across multiple workflows
- prevent repeated incidents through better design
- mentor junior and mid engineers effectively

## Exit Condition

There is no hard finish line here.
You remain at this level by producing strong outcomes repeatedly over time.
