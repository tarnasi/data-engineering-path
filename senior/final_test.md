# Senior Final Test

This is not a simple exam.
It is a proof of repeated capability.

## Final Evaluation

Complete a major system design or improvement that includes all of the following:

1. architecture definition
2. tradeoff analysis
3. reliability and observability plan
4. cost awareness
5. governance or access considerations
6. documentation others can maintain
7. evidence that the system or team improved

## Required Explanation

You should be able to explain:

- why this design is the right fit for the problem
- what you chose not to build and why
- where the system will fail first under pressure
- how incidents will be detected and handled
- how the team can operate the system safely
- what long-term risks still remain

## Pass Standard

You are operating at Senior mastery when your designs hold up in practice, your team trusts your judgment, and your work raises the quality of the platform around you.
